Vesak Day Coffin Donation Drive

Helping the Less Fortunate Have a Dignified Farewell

On this sacred Vesak Day, a time for reflection, compassion, and giving, Toa Payoh Seu Teck Sean Tong urges Singaporeans to come forward and support our Coffin Donation Drive.

Why This Drive is Urgent

In recent months, Singapore has seen a rise in elderly citizens passing away without children or financial support to provide for their funeral arrangements. With inflation on the rise and the 9% GST further burdening households, many elderly individuals have been left without aid or proper send-offs.

For decades, Toa Payoh Seu Teck Sean Tong has stood as a pillar of compassion, ensuring that the less fortunate receive dignified final rites. However, funds have run dangerously low, and we now struggle to continue providing aid. Without support, many deceased individuals may go without a proper burial, their final journey left incomplete.

How You Can Help

We call upon kind-hearted Singaporeans to step forward and donate. Your contributions will go directly towards purchasing coffins and arranging proper funeral services for those in need.

  • Every donation counts, no matter how big or small.
  • Donating earns you spiritual merits, bringing blessings and good fortune.
  • Your generosity ensures the elderly receive the dignity they deserve in their final moments.

The Power of Good Deeds

Vesak Day is about compassion, kindness, and doing good for others. By giving, you create a cycle of positive energy, ensuring your future is filled with blessings, smooth paths, and good luck.
